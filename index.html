What’s The Game

So Color Wood Jam is basically this logic puzzle where you're moving these colored wooden blocks around to clear paths and finish levels.

You gotta think through your moves instead of racing the clock - each puzzle's like a mini-challenge that won't make you wanna chuck your phone across the room.

How To Play

  • Slide wooden blocks across the board

  • Match colors to unlock paths

  • Free blocked elements step by step

  • Complete the level using logical sequencing

Difficulty Flow

Short and clear early levels for onboarding.
Gradual increase in complexity. More colors and obstacles over time.

Visual Style
